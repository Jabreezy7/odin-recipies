Cereal Recipe

Description

This is an exquisite dish that only a few can truly appreciate. If you're here then you must be one of those lucky few.

This is a recipe that should only take you 30-60 seconds to complete, and utilizes only 2 ingredients. Remember simple is best.

Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Cereal

Steps

  1. Grab a medium sized bowl
  2. Fill the bowl 3/4's full of your desired cereal (I suggest Cinnamon Toast Crunch)
  3. Pour milk into the bowl (don't drown your cereal with milk please)
